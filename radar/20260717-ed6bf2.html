New York Bans Hyperscale AI Data Centers for One Year

New York Governor Kathy Hochul signed an executive order imposing a one-year moratorium on the construction of hyperscale AI data centers using more than 50 megawatts of power — the first such ban in the US. The order also requires data centers to produce their own energy or pay a premium for grid power, and removes their exemption from local zoning laws. The move signals growing community and regulatory pushback against AI infrastructure expansion.

The moratorium covers data centers using more than 50 megawatts of power and is the first of its kind in the US
