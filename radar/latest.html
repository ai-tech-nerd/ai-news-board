Apple Sues OpenAI Over Hardware Trade Secret Theft

Apple filed a federal lawsuit against OpenAI, its io devices unit, and two former Apple employees — hardware chief Tang Tan and engineer Chang Liu — alleging a coordinated campaign to poach staff and steal confidential hardware secrets. The suit claims employees were coached to bring physical Apple parts to interviews and shown how to circumvent Apple security protocols to exfiltrate files. OpenAI denied the claims, stating it has 'no interest in other companies' trade secrets.'

OpenAI has hired over 400 former Apple employees, including hardware chief Tang Tan who worked at Apple for 24 years.
