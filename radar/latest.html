Meta

Meta Launches Muse Image, Ranks #2 on Arena Leaderboard

Meta's Superintelligence Labs released Muse Image, its first in-house image generation model, which immediately ranked #2 on Arena's text-to-image and editing leaderboards, trailing only OpenAI's GPT Image 2. The model is rolling out across Meta AI, Instagram, and WhatsApp, with Facebook and Messenger coming later. Meta also teased an upcoming Muse Video model, which previewed at #3 on the video leaderboard.

Muse Image ranked #2 on Arena's text-to-image and editing leaderboards, behind only GPT Image 2

Microsoft

Microsoft Replaces OpenAI/Anthropic with In-House MAI Models

Microsoft announced it is swapping out OpenAI and Anthropic models in products like Excel and Outlook with its own internally built MAI models, primarily to cut licensing costs. Tens of thousands of AI prompts in those apps are now handled weekly by MAI models. This follows Microsoft AI Chief Mustafa Suleiman's stated intention to reduce dependence on third-party models.

Tens of thousands of AI prompts in Excel and Outlook are now handled each week by Microsoft's internally built MAI models

#3
Bytedance

Beijing Considers Restricting Chinese AI Model Exports

China's commerce officials are reportedly discussing rules that would limit foreign access to the country's most powerful AI models, including Qwen (Alibaba), Doubao (ByteDance), and GLM-5.2 (Zhipu AI). The discussions include both closed and open models, startup funding limits, and penalties for leaking proprietary AI. This mirrors export control moves made by Washington with Anthropic's Fable and Mythos models.

China's discussions would affect Qwen, Doubao, and GLM-5.2 — models that gained global users through low costs and strong performance

Cloudflare

Cloudflare Launches Monetization Gateway for Agent Internet

Cloudflare announced its Monetization Gateway, a platform that lets website owners put any digital resource — web pages, datasets, APIs, or MCP tools — behind a usage-based paywall for AI agents. The announcement garnered over 4 million views, reflecting the urgency of the problem it addresses. Cloudflare reported a 1,700% jump in daily AI agent requests from 2025 to 2026.

Cloudflare reports a 1,700% jump in daily AI agent requests from 2025 to 2026, with over 50% of today's internet traffic being non-human

Anthropic

Anthropic Upgrades Claude Cowork to Cross-Device Platform

Anthropic made Claude Cowork available across web and mobile, allowing users to start tasks on desktop, receive updates on mobile, and access results from any device. The update also introduces 'scheduled tasks' that run even when the user's computer is off. The rollout begins with Max subscribers, and Anthropic also extended access to Fable 5 on paid plans through July 12.

Anthropic's usage report from 1.2 million anonymized Claude Cowork sessions found business operations is the top use case at 33.4% of sessions

Digicert

AI Adoption Outpacing Enterprise Security, DigiCert Research Finds

New research from security firm DigiCert, surveying 1,001 IT and cybersecurity leaders across the US, UK, and Australia, found that nearly 80% of enterprises have encountered AI-related security issues in the past six months. Approximately 50% experienced at least one security incident caused by an unauthorized or misconfigured AI agent. Science and technology organizations reported the highest rates of incidents.

50% of enterprises surveyed experienced at least one security incident caused by an unauthorized or misconfigured AI agent

Microsoft Cuts 4,800 Jobs While Expanding AI Division

Microsoft eliminated approximately 4,800 jobs, representing 2.1% of its workforce, on Monday. Chief People Officer Amy Coleman stated the cuts are not due to AI replacing those roles. However, Microsoft is simultaneously shifting resources to expand its AI division, reflecting the broader industry pattern of AI investment funded by workforce reductions.
